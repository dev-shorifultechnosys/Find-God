How To Share

A simple conversation guide for using the FIND GOD salvation card with peace and confidence.

Simple Conversation

You Do Not Need to Argue, Pressure, or Debate

Let Scripture speak. The card helps guide the conversation one step at a time, so the person can hear the message clearly and respond freely.

Download the Card

Ask permission

“Would you mind if I shared something with you that has helped me understand God’s love?”

Read the card together

Let the message stay simple and centered on Scripture.

Ask the simple questions

The card helps guide the conversation one step at a time.

Invite them to respond

If they are ready, they can pray to receive Jesus Christ as Savior.

Help them take the next step

Encourage prayer, Bible reading, worship, and connection with a Bible-teaching church.

Carry the Card. Pray for an Opportunity. Begin Simply.

The goal is not to win an argument. The goal is to lovingly share the good news of Jesus Christ.
