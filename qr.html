You Are Loved By God

If someone gave you a FIND GOD card, they wanted you to know the good news of Jesus Christ.

God loves you. Sin separates us from God. Jesus died on the cross for our sins and rose again. Everyone who believes in Him can receive forgiveness, salvation, and eternal life.

A Simple Prayer

Dear God, I have sinned against You. I ask forgiveness for all my sins. I believe Jesus died on the cross for my sins and rose again. Father, I surrender my life to Your will. I ask Jesus to come into my life and trust in Him alone. I pray this in Jesus’ name, amen.
