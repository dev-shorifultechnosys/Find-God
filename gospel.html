The Gospel

A simple, clear, biblical explanation of the good news of Jesus Christ.

The Good News

God Loves You

The Gospel begins with God’s love. God created us to know Him, walk with Him, and live in relationship with Him.

Sin separates us from God. The problem is not that people only need improvement. We need forgiveness, salvation, and new life.

Jesus died on the cross for our sins and rose again.

Sin and Forgiveness

FIND GOD helps people explain why forgiveness is needed and why Jesus Christ is the only Savior.

Faith and Salvation

Everyone who believes in Him can receive forgiveness, salvation, and eternal life.

Next Steps
